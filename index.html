Your exercise requirements this week were to:

  • Find a utility pole in your neighbourhood that has some additional equipment attached to it. For example: a fill-in 5G cell system, a fibre network distribution box or even just a transformer.
  • Take a clear picture of the equipment. Crop the image to just the utility equipment.
  • Create a plain Winlink message addressed to CLACKAMAS-WEEKLY NET and add your check-in sentence.
  • Describe (briefly) where the image was taken from and roughly the direction you were facing.
  • Include any pertinent information. For example, the utility pole number (often on an affixed metal plate), anything that cannot be seen in your image.
  • Attach your image to the plain Winlink message. Again, your goal is to get this image to 100 kbytes. Use the WLE image scaling tool to do this.

Note that if an AUXC checked in through multiple RMSs, then each check-in sentence is provided at the top of their information.
